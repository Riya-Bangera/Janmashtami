## Public User Guide

### How to Register for Competitions

1. **Start Registration**
   - Click "Start Registration" on the home page
   - Ensure registration is open (check with admin if closed)

2. **Step 1: Profile Information**
   - Enter your full name
   - Select your date of birth
   - Click "Next"
   - Your age and category will be automatically calculated

3. **Step 2: Event Selection**
   - View competitions available for your age group
   - Select one or more competitions you want to participate in
   - Review the time and fee for each competition
   - Click "Next"

4. **Step 3: Payment**
   - View the total fee
   - Scan the UPI QR code with your payment app
   - Complete the payment
   - Upload a screenshot of the payment confirmation
   - Click "Complete Registration"

5. **Registration Confirmation**
   - Save your Registration ID (shown as QR code and text)
   - Click "Download Receipt" to print your confirmation
   - Bring this receipt on the day of the event

### Viewing Winners

1. Click "View Winners" on the home page
2. Browse through published competition results
3. See the top 3 winners for each competition

## Admin Guide

### Login
- Username: `admin`
- Password: `admin123`
- Navigate to Admin Login from the home page

### Dashboard Overview
View real-time statistics:
- Total Participants
- Total Revenue
- Confirmed Registrations

### Managing Registrations

1. **View All Registrations**
   - Click "Registration Management"
   - Use filters to find specific registrations:
     - Filter by Age Group
     - Filter by Competition

2. **Add On-Spot Registration**
   - Click "On-Spot Registration"
   - Enter participant details
   - Select date of birth
   - Choose competitions
   - Select payment method (Cash/Online)
   - Click "Add Registration"

### Managing Staff

1. **Add New Staff**
   - Click "Staff Management"
   - Click "Add Staff Member"
   - Enter username and password
   - Select role (Judge or Host)
   - For judges: Assign specific competitions
   - Click "Add Staff Member"

2. **Edit Staff**
   - Click the edit icon next to any staff member
   - Update details as needed
   - Click "Update Staff Member"

3. **Delete Staff**
   - Click the delete icon next to any staff member
   - Confirm deletion

### Managing Competitions

1. **Add New Competition**
   - Click "Competition Management"
   - Click "Add Competition"
   - Enter competition name and time
   - Set base fee and additional fee
   - Select applicable age groups
   - Add scoring rubrics (criteria and max scores)
   - Click "Add Competition"

2. **Delete Competition**
   - Click the delete icon next to any competition
   - Confirm deletion

### System Settings

1. **Toggle Registration**
   - Click "System Settings"
   - Use the switch to open/close registration
   - When closed, public users cannot register

2. **Update UPI ID**
   - Enter your UPI ID
   - Preview the QR code that will be shown to participants
   - Click "Save Settings"

## Judge Guide

### Login
- Use credentials provided by admin
- Navigate to Judge Login from the home page

### Scoring Participants

1. **Select Competition**
   - Choose a competition from your assigned list
   - View all registered participants

2. **Score a Participant**
   - Click "Score" next to a participant's name
   - Enter scores for each rubric (criteria)
   - Scores must be within the maximum allowed
   - Click "Submit Score"

3. **Update Scores**
   - Click "Score" again on any participant
   - Modify the scores
   - Click "Submit Score" to update

### Publishing Results

1. **View Score Matrix**
   - Switch to "Results & Publishing" tab
   - View ranked participants with average scores
   - See how many judges have scored each participant

2. **Select Winners**
   - Click "Publish Results"
   - Select 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place winners
   - Winners are suggested based on average scores
   - Click "Publish Results"

3. **Important Notes**
   - Results are only visible to hosts and public after publishing
   - Ensure all judges have completed scoring before publishing
   - Published results appear in the Hall of Fame

## Host Guide

### Login
- Use credentials provided by admin
- Navigate to Host Login from the home page

### Managing Participant Queue

1. **Select Competition**
   - Choose a competition from the dropdown
   - View all registered participants

2. **Call Participants to Stage**
   - Review the participant queue
   - Click "Call to Stage" for the next participant
   - Participant status changes to "Called"
   - Continue calling participants as needed

### Announcing Winners

1. **Check for Published Results**
   - Click "Show Winners" button (appears when results are published)
   - View the Divine Ranks celebration screen

2. **Divine Ranks Display**
   - Large, celebratory display of winners
   - Shows 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place
   - Use this screen for public announcements
   - Click "Back to Queue" to return to participant management

## Tips and Best Practices

### For Participants
- Register early to secure your spot
- Keep your registration ID safe
- Arrive 30 minutes before your competition time
- Bring a printed copy of your receipt

### For Admins
- Set up all competitions before opening registration
- Add judges and hosts early
- Test the UPI QR code before going live
- Monitor registrations regularly
- Close registration when capacity is reached

### For Judges
- Review rubrics before scoring begins
- Be consistent in your scoring
- Score all participants before publishing results
- Coordinate with other judges for fairness
- Double-check winner selection before publishing

### For Hosts
- Familiarize yourself with the participant list
- Call participants in order
- Keep the event moving smoothly
- Use the Divine Ranks screen for dramatic winner reveals
- Coordinate with judges on timing

## Troubleshooting

### Registration Issues
- **Registration Closed**: Contact admin to open registration
- **Payment QR Code Not Working**: Verify UPI ID in settings
- **Can't Upload Screenshot**: Check file size and format

### Login Issues
- **Invalid Credentials**: Verify username and password with admin
- **Can't Access Dashboard**: Ensure you're using the correct login page for your role

### Data Issues
- **Missing Registrations**: Check browser localStorage
- **Lost Data**: Data is stored locally; clearing browser data will erase it
- **Sync Issues**: Each browser/device has separate data storage
