🎯 KEY FEATURES:

   1. PARENT/GUARDIAN INFORMATION
      • Parent's full name (required)
      • Parent's phone number (required, 10 digits)
      • Validation for phone format
      • Mandatory for all registrations

   2. PAYMENT VERIFICATION
      • Payment amount input field
      • Payment timestamp input field
      • Amount must match total fee exactly
      • Timestamp validation:
        - Cannot be in future
        - Must be within last 7 days
      • Clear error messages for verification failures

   3. PDF RECEIPT GENERATION
      • Professional PDF format using jsPDF
      • Includes all registration details
      • Participant information
      • Parent/guardian details
      • Competition list with times
      • Payment verification details
      • Downloadable with one click
      • Filename: Registration_[ID].pdf
