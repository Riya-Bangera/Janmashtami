# Winner Selection Quick Guide

## For Judges: How to Select and Publish Winners

### Step 1: Complete Scoring ✅
- Score all participants in your assigned competitions
- Check that all other judges have also completed scoring
- Look for green "Scoring Complete" alert

### Step 2: Review Rankings 📊
- Go to "Results & Publishing" tab
- Check "Participants Ranking" table
- Participants are sorted by average score (highest first)
- Verify scores look correct

### Step 3: Select Winners 🏆
- Click "Select Winners" button
- Choose participants for each rank:
  - 🥇 1st Place
  - 🥈 2nd Place
  - 🥉 3rd Place
- Participants show their average scores

### Step 4: Save or Publish 💾
**Option A: Save as Draft**
- Click "Save as Draft"
- Winners saved but not public
- Can edit anytime
- Use this to prepare results

**Option B: Publish Results**
- Click "Publish Results"
- Winners become public immediately
- Visible to hosts and public
- Can still edit later if needed

---

## Scoring Status Indicators

### ✅ Green Alert: "Scoring Complete"
- All judges have scored all participants
- You can publish results
- Publish button is enabled

### ⚠️ Orange Alert: "Scoring Incomplete"
- Some scores are missing
- Shows count of missing scores
- Can save draft, but cannot publish
- Publish button is disabled

---

## Winner Status Badges

### 🟢 Published (Green Badge)
- Results are public
- Visible in Hall of Fame
- Visible to hosts
- Can still be edited

### 🟠 Draft (Orange Badge)
- Results saved but not public
- Only visible to judges
- Not in Hall of Fame yet
- Can be edited freely

---

## Quick Actions

### To Edit Winners
1. Click "Edit Winners" button
2. Change selections
3. Click "Save as Draft" or "Publish Results"

### To Check Scoring Progress
1. Go to "Results & Publishing" tab
2. Look at "Judges Scoring Progress" section
3. Each judge shows progress bar and percentage

### To Publish Draft Results
1. Ensure scoring is complete (green alert)
2. Open winner selection dialog
3. Click "Publish Results"

---

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I change winners after publishing?**
A: Yes! Open the dialog and re-publish with new selections.

**Q: Why is publish button disabled?**
A: Not all judges have scored all participants yet. Check progress bars.

**Q: What's the difference between Save and Publish?**
A: Save = private draft. Publish = public and visible to everyone.

**Q: Can I save winners before all scoring is done?**
A: Yes! Use "Save as Draft" to prepare results early.

**Q: Where do published results appear?**
A: In Host Dashboard (Divine Ranks) and public Hall of Fame page.
